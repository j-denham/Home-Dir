~~ COMPARISONS ~~

{Numeric || String}

Less Than: -lt || <
Greater Than: -gt || >
Equality: -eq || =
Not Equal: -ne | !=
Less or Equal: -le || N/A
Greater or Equal: -ge || N/A

0 evaluates to TRUE, 1 evaluates to FALSE

~~ FUNCTION PARAMETERS ~~

$1 for first argument, $2 for second argument, $n for nth argument
$# for total number of arguments
$* for all arguments in order
